About our Machine

The machine automates the process of watering the vermicompost through the use solenoid valve, the water flow is enabled and disabled by the solenoid water valves.The machine features a two worm bins for faster worm migration. The first bin was prepared for the first vermicomposting. The second bin was made ready for worm migration for the next vermicomposting.There is also a divider between the two bins that will open during the worm migration. To assure the transferring of worms between the bins, a lamp will light up during worm migration.

Traditional Vermicomposting

The manual method of vermicomposting involves creating a controlled environment,where earthworms transform organic waste into nutrient-rich compost. Key steps include setting up a suitable container with proper ventilation,, introducing a bedding material for the worms. Maintenance involves monitoring moisture levels, turning the contents for aeration, and harvesting nutrient-rich castings produced by the earthworms..
